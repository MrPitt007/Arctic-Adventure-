How to Get Here

The closest airport is Luleå Airport (LLA). From there:

Mode of Transport Details Estimated Price
Airport Shuttle (Flygbilen) Shared minibus from Luleå Airport to Övertorneå ≈ 385 SEK one way
Taxi / Private Car Direct from Luleå Airport to Övertorneå (~153 km) ~240–280 SEK

Travel time by car: ~1h 40min.
